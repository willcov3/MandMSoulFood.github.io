Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the original M&M Soul Food?

Yes! We are the original M&M Soul Food. Though many have tried to duplicate, only one can originate - Be Original. Since 1973

What's popular on the menu?

We recommend that first time visitors try our fried chicken, ox tails, or fried fish. The top 3 sides are collard greens, candied yams, and macaroni & cheese. Also try our Twist or Polar Blast made with fresh squeezed lemonade and fruit punch, and don't forget our deserts made fresh daily!


Are you cash only?

No. We accept cash, credit, and debit.


Can I make a reservation to dine in?

No. Dine in is first come, first serve!


Are you hiring?

We welcome all to come in and apply. Dishwasher and Assistant Cashier spots are open.


Why is the phone line always busy?

If you call our restuarant, and the phone line is busy try again in a few minutes. A busy signal means that all phone lines currently have a caller on them. You can help reduce this problem by having your order ready when you call.


How long does it take for to go orders?

Depending on the time and day of the week, orders will take 15 minutes to an hour to prepare. Fried and grilled foods take the longest to prepare.


I received the incorrect order/ missing plates from my order. What should I do?

Always check your bags before you leave! Give us a call at (310) 324-5317, and we will do our best to rectify the order.


I was not satisfied with my purchase. Can I get my money back?

We are happy to remake or refund any food that you are not satisfied with as long as you return the food. M&Ms does not refund or remake food that has already been eaten. If it is a to go order, YOU MUST BRING FOOD BACK.


What delivery and online ordering services do you offer?

Delivery and online ordering is available through UberEats for pick up and delivery.
