Location

Venni Mac's M & M Soul Food Restaurant is located in Carson, adjacent to the 91 Freeway at 335 East Albertoni Street. Ste. 300. We have been at this location since 2006. M&M's provides southern home-style cooking with southern elegance and decorum, giving customers the comfort of a home cooked meal in a family style environment.
